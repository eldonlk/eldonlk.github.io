About Eldon (black)

A suprise to most, I am actually the one in the black.

I am currently finishing up my Statistics Master's Program at UC Berkeley. Before, I graduated from USC with a degree in Economics and Statistics.

After graduating undergrad, I worked at PAAMCO Prisma, a fund of hedge funds. I began as a Risk Data Analyst, covering the entire data pipeline all while building statistical risk models and making visual reports to all levels of the firm. Soon after, I was moved to a team of software developers to help create porfolio and risk monitoring software that would be sold externally. Luckily, I was able to get exposure at every level of the process. This sparked my interest in technology leading me to go back to school to earn a higher degree in statistics. I becam entralled in the interaction between people an technology.

When I have free time, I enjoy going to the gym, hiking, biking, photography and all sports. I am a huge Laker's and Dodgers fan and really felt like this was their year. I'm always looking to new places to eat, things to do, movies to watch.
